Here are the main points of our product implementation:


The weight information is transmitted through the PCB to the main core of our system, which is the Raspberry Pi 4 computer.

We use the Raspberry Pi 4 Model B Camera module and the YOLO library to identify the product placed on the scale.

The database loaded in Raspberry Pi 4 provides the price of one kilogram of the identified product.

The touch screen connected to the Raspberry Pi 4 displays the total weight of the product, the price of one kilogram, and the total price of the selected product for the buyer to see.

Our product is designed to simplify the buying process for customers, providing accurate and reliable weight and price information while offering a convenient and secure payment method.
