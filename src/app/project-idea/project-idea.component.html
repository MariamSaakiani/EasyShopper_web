Where did the idea come from?
When we started thinking about the idea, we thought about all the everyday inconviniences that we could solve with this project. This is where the idea came from, which would allow the customer to weight the product himself, and find out the price independently.

What is our idea?
Our idea is to create a device that will be a smart scale with additional functions that will eliminate the need for consultant to remember all the prices or codes of the products and enter it into the device.

What are the pros of implementing this idea?
The result of the implementation of this project will be to simplify, speed up the buying and selling process and make the work of the employed personal easier.

What is general principle of operation of the device?
Let's imagine the following scenario: we take the product, any fruit or vegetable in teh desired quantity, we place it on the scale board, this device will have a camera that percieves the product, identifies it, then the rate, weight, and the final amount to be paid are displayed on the screen.
